Welcome to the Class Homepage

This page contains selected materials for a course, Landscape Analysis and Modeling at The University of Tulsa, (BIOL 4383/6383), developed and taught by Michael Treglia, funded by the Oklahoma NSF-EPSCoR Program (Grant No. IIA-1301789).

The syllabus is available here. Materials for Lab Exercises and links to readings are provided in the Schedule. Generally, lecture materials are not made publically available here at this time.
